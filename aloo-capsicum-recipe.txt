Ingredients: 
 - 1 Potato
 - 1 Capsicum
 - 1 Onion
 - 1 Popu

Directions: 
 1. Heat oil on stove on medium flame

 2. Once the oil is warm enough, add the Popu. 

 3. Next add the onions and fry it until they are golden brown in color. 

 4. Next add potato and fry it until it is properly and evenly cooked. 

 5. Finally add capsicum and fry it until is properly cooked along with others. 

 Most delicious aloo-capsicum-curry is ready to be served.
